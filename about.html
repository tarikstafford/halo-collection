Our Story

A new chapter in hospitality, rooted in 20 years of warmth and care

Hello and welcome! I'm Sharona Hurmuses, and together with my husband Jeff, we're the proud co-founders of The Halo Collection—a heartfelt continuation of our journey in hospitality, now rooted in the vibrant neighborhoods of Athens.

From Mykonos to Athens

For over 20 years, we poured our souls into Villa Hurmuses in Mykonos, creating a place where guests felt not just welcomed, but genuinely cared for. When it came time to say goodbye to that beloved villa, we knew we weren't finished—we were simply ready for a new chapter. And so, The Halo Collection was born.

Three Homes, Three Stories

In 2024, we launched three thoughtfully designed boutique apartments and home: The Hive in Kypseli, full of vintage charm and buzzing city life; The Hill, a quiet, cozy nest tucked near the foothills of Lykavittos; and The Silk House in Akidemia Platonos, where creative spirit and old-world textures meet. Each space is a reflection of who we are—warm, stylish, and just a little bit unexpected.

About Sharona

My background as a former Singapore Airlines Flight Attendant and Brand Ambassador instilled in me a deep love for service, details, and the power of a personal touch.

About Jeff

Jeff brings his Greek-Canadian roots, his builder's eye and his calm, grounded energy to everything we do. With a meticulous eye for detail and a practical mindset, he ensures each project is not only beautiful but thoughtfully executed from the ground up.

We're also parents to three incredible daughters, which keeps life full, fun, and wonderfully unpredictable.

Our Mission

We've created homes that welcome guests not just as visitors, but as friends. Whether you're here for a weekend escape or an extended stay, our goal is simple: to make you feel at ease, inspired, and at home. The Halo Collection isn't just about beautiful places—it's about connection, comfort, and the little moments that make travel magical.

We can't wait to welcome you.

— Sharona & Jeff Hurmuses
